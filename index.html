About Me

Design with
heart & purpose

I'm a fresh UI design graduate from Noroff, all about creating clean, accessible, and user-friendly designs. I love playing with color and imagery to build experiences that are joyful and motivating.

Before this career change, I spent 10 wonderful years as a kindergarten teacher — which gave me deep empathy, patience, and clear communication skills that translate directly into great UI design. When I'm not designing, I'm with my two daughters and fiancé, enjoying nature walks, jogging, kayaking, or reading.
